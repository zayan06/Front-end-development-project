Right of Way Rules

Who goes first? Right of way rules prevent accidents when traffic lights fail or don't exist.

1. The Golden Rule of Intersections At a 4-way stop, the vehicle that arrives first gets to go first.
Car A arrives at 10:00:01.
Car B arrives at 10:00:05.
Result: Car A goes first.
2. The "Right" Rule If two vehicles arrive at the exact same time, the vehicle on the right has the right of way.
YOU are at the stop line.
A TRUCK is to your right.
Result: You wait. The Truck goes first.
3. Left Turns Yield If you are turning left, you must yield to oncoming traffic going straight.
[Green Light]
Oncoming car is going straight.
Result: You must wait in the intersection until they pass.
4. Pedestrians Always Win Pedestrians in a crosswalk always have the right of way, regardless of lights or signs.
